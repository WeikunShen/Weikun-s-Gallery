Captain Octopus

Captain Octopus is a notorious pirate of this era. No one knows his last name, nor do they know where he comes from. To be more accurate, he's like a ghost, or perhaps he is one himself?

Undoubtedly, wherever Captain Octopus and his crew go, treasures are swept away. Not only that, after their visits, octopuses caught by the local fishermen mysteriously disappear. Perhaps they have a fondness for this type of food? Or maybe they keep them as pets, like the one on his hat?
